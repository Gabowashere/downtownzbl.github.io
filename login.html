Brand

Founded in 2024 in the heart of Manila, DTZL is a dynamic streetwear brand born from the shared vision of six like-minded founders: Jurex, Steven, Gabriel, Gab, Roy, and Kean. United by their passion for urban culture, creativity, and individuality, they set out to craft bold, high-quality apparel that reflects the energy and diversity of the streets. DTZL embodies a lifestyle of self-expression, pushing boundaries, and connecting communities through fashion.
